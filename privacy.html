Privacy Policy of Funny Math!

Funny Math! — the app for training a verbal arithmetic account.

This app doesn’t required and request any of your personal data or information, including children. All information you enter into the app is not transmitted from your device and is only stored locally on your device. Also, this app doesn’t include third-party advertising or third-party analytics.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

If you have any questions or suggestions, do not hesitate to contact us via our Facebook page.
